Our precious puffins

Puffins are those weird birds that look like a flying penguin. They are seabird species that belong to the auk family.

There's not just one kind of puffin, there are 4 species of puffins such as:

  • Atlantic puffin

  • Horned puffin

  • Tufted puffin

  • Rhinoceros Auklet

Info about puffins

  1. In spring and summer thousands of puffins gather in colonies on the coast and North Atlantic Ocean to breed.

  2. Each year, puffins lay an average of one egg, usually staying with the same mate throughout their lifetimes.

  3. One funny thing is that their babies are called pufflings.

  4. They can fly at 88km/h beating their wings up to 400 times a minute.
